# Reverse To-Do — Evening Ledger Design System

## Intent
Quiet evening journal. Facts, not plans. Calm typography, minimal chrome.

## Typography
- Display: Instrument Serif (dates, questions, insights)
- Body/UI: DM Sans
- Stats: DM Sans tabular-nums

## Colors (OKLCH)
- canvas / parchment background
- ink hierarchy (primary, muted, whisper)
- ember accent for done/streak
- category: slate=work, honey=learning, sage=health

## Signature
Washi Card — soft paper surface, thin border, category accent stripe. Entries, composer, stats, and week blocks each get a small card.

## Anti-patterns
No todo checkboxes, KPI card grids, pie charts, motivational copy, Inter+purple gradient.
